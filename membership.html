What is Lovers'Vow?

Lovers'Vow is a premium dating platform designed to help singles find meaningful connections and lasting relationships. We use advanced matching algorithms and provide a safe, verified environment for our members to connect.

What kind of photos can I use?

We encourage members to use clear, recent photos that represent themselves authentically. Photos should be appropriate and follow our community guidelines. We verify all profile photos to ensure authenticity and maintain a safe environment for all members.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and various regional payment methods depending on your location. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels to protect your financial information.

How does your matchmaking work?

Our matchmaking algorithm considers multiple factors including your profile information, preferences, interests, and behavior on the platform. We use machine learning to continuously improve our matching accuracy and suggest compatible partners based on shared values and compatibility indicators.

How can I delete my profile?

You can delete your profile at any time by going to your account settings and selecting "Delete Account". Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our systems in accordance with our privacy policy.
